2. Explore Entrance Exams

Science Stream Entrance Exams:

NEET-UG

National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (Undergraduate) for admission to MBBS/BDS courses.

JEE Main

Joint Entrance Examination (Main) for admission to undergraduate engineering programs.

JEE Advanced

For admission to Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) after clearing JEE Main.

ICAR AIEEA

Indian Council of Agricultural Research All India Entrance Examination for Agriculture.

JENPAS UG

Joint Entrance Test for Nursing, Paramedical, and Allied Sciences Undergraduate Courses (West Bengal).

JEXPO

Joint Entrance Examination for Polytechnic (West Bengal) for diploma courses.

JELET

Joint Entrance Lateral Entry Test (West Bengal) for lateral entry to B.Tech courses.

Arts Stream Entrance Exams:

CLAT

Common Law Admission Test for undergraduate and postgraduate law programs.

AILET

All India Law Entrance Test for BA-LLB (Hons.), LLM, and PhD programs at NLU Delhi.

CUET UG (Arts)

Common University Entrance Test for various undergraduate programs in Arts at Central Universities.

NIFT Entrance Exam

For admission to design, fashion technology, and management programs at NIFT.

NID DAT

National Institute of Design Design Aptitude Test for various design programs.

BFA Entrance Exams

Various entrance exams for Bachelor of Fine Arts programs in different universities.

4. Competitive Government Job Exams

For those aspiring to a career in the government sector, here are some of the top competitive exams in India, along with probable posts and approximate salaries.

UPSC Civil Services Exam (CSE)

Posts: IAS, IPS, IFS, IRS, etc.

Approx. Salary: ₹56,100 - ₹2,50,000+ (per month, varies by post & experience)

SSC CGL

Posts: Assistant Audit Officer, Income Tax Inspector, Excise Inspector, ASO, etc.

Approx. Salary: ₹35,400 - ₹1,51,100 (per month, varies by post & grade pay)

SSC CHSL

Posts: LDC, JSA, PA, SA, DEO

Approx. Salary: ₹19,900 - ₹81,100 (per month, varies by post & grade pay)

SSC MTS

Posts: Multi-Tasking Staff (non-technical)

Approx. Salary: ₹18,000 - ₹56,900 (per month, varies by post & grade pay)

RRB NTPC

Posts: Junior Clerk cum Typist, Commercial cum Ticket Clerk, Goods Guard, Station Master, etc.

Approx. Salary: ₹19,900 - ₹35,400 (per month, varies by post)

RRB Group D

Posts: Track Maintainer Grade-IV, Helper/Assistant, Hospital Assistant, etc.

Approx. Salary: ₹18,000 (per month, Level 1 of 7th CPC Pay Matrix)

RRB ALP

Posts: Assistant Loco Pilot, Technicians

Approx. Salary: ₹19,900 (per month, Level 2 of 7th CPC Pay Matrix)

State PCS Exams

Posts: Deputy Collector, DSP, Block Development Officer (State-specific)

Approx. Salary: Varies by state and post, generally similar to UPSC entry-level posts.
